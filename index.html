Tokenomics

10% of every $DGC trade and transaction is taken as a "transfer tax". Here's how the transfer tax is distributed:

5 %

Treasury

The treasury is used to strategically trade very low-cap altcoins and return profits to holders as dividends.

3 %

Instant Dividends

Unlike manual dividends which are occasionally paid out by the treasury, instant dividends are immediately claimable by holders.

1 %

Liquidity Pool

A small percentage of every transaction is set aside to strengthen the liquidity pool, enabling larger trade sizes and tighter spreads.

1 %

Developers

A sliver of each transaction is paid out to the developers, for covering operational costs and incentivizing treasury management.
